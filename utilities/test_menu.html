The Global Predisposition Indicator (GPITM)

About the GPI

  • The GPI helps people assess their behavioural predispositions and develop an understanding of their natural or preferred style of behaviour
  • It is less about what people do and more about who they are in terms of:
    • Problem Solving and Implementation
    • Communication and Interpersonal Style
    • Feelings and Self control
    • Entrepreneurship Style
    • Career drivers
    • Relationships
    • Leadership
  • The GPI was developed by Dr S Glowinkowski in the early 90s. Many thousands of senior global leaders have completed the GPI questionnaire and received in-depth feedback
  • The GPI helps people establish clarity for themselves, achieve potential, improve their performance and build confidence
  • It is used in the following context:
    • Individual coaching
    • Leadership development
    • Team-working
    • Recruitment
    • Culture change in organisations
    • Careers guidance
    • The school and learning environment
    • Talent management
    • Succession planning
    • Family counselling
    • Relationship building
